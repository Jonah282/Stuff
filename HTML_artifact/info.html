What is a Car?

A car is a wheeled motor vehicle used for transportation. Most definitions state that cars run primarily on roads, seat one to eight people, have four wheels, and mainly transport people rather than goods.

History of Cars

The first practical automobile was invented by Karl Benz in 1885. Since then, cars have evolved significantly, incorporating new technologies and becoming more efficient and safer.
